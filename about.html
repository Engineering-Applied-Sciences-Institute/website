About us

Our mission, values, and focus.

Who we are

EASI is an independent nonprofit research institute. We pursue research, development, and education in the fields of engineering and applied sciences for the benefit of the public. Our work emphasizes modeling, estimation, and control of complex uncertain systems.

Our mission

To engage in research, development, and education in the fields of engineering and applied sciences for the benefit of the public.

We ask: How can rigorous modeling and control of complex uncertain systems enable new capabilities in space systems, robotics and autonomous systems, and applications of AI/ML?

Our values

Technical rigor

We use sound methodology, clear assumptions, and reproducible practices so that our work can be verified and built upon.

Innovation

We pursue novel approaches and collaborations to advance the state of the art in our focus areas.

Transparency

We share methods, document assumptions, and publish in accessible forms. Where possible we make data, code, and tools available to support replication and reuse.
