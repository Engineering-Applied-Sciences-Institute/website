About us

Our mission, values, and commitment to independent research in the public interest.

Our mission

To engage in research, development, and education in the fields of engineering and applied sciences for the benefit of the public.

EASI pursues this mission through work that emphasizes modeling, estimation, and control of complex uncertain systems. We aim to expand into additional domains over time while keeping our focus on rigorous, publicly beneficial outcomes.

Our values

Independence

We do not accept funding that would compromise our ability to report findings honestly. Our conclusions are our own.

Rigor

We use transparent methods, peer review, and open data practices so that our work can be verified and built upon.

Access

We publish reports, briefs, and tools in formats that are useful to decision-makers and the general public.

Our focus

A lot of what we do centers on modeling, estimation, and control of complex uncertain systems—from space systems to potential applications in biology and medicine. We plan to grow into other domains in the future while maintaining our commitment to research, development, and education for the public benefit.
