Our Research Get in Touch

Our mission

To engage in research, development, and education in the fields of engineering and applied sciences for the benefit of the public.

Research

We pursue rigorous work in modeling, estimation, and control of complex uncertain systems, with plans to expand into additional domains.

Development

From space systems to biomedical applications, we translate ideas into prototypes and solutions that serve the public interest.

Education

We share our methods and findings to train the next generation and support practitioners in the field.

Featured research

Our current projects span space systems, dynamics, and computational biology.

Satellite-to-satellite power beaming

Research and development of wireless power beaming between spacecraft in orbit, enabling new capabilities for space infrastructure.

Dynamics of tumbling objects

Characterizing the dynamics of tumbling objects to support modeling, estimation, and control of complex uncertain systems in space and related domains.

Computational biology for medical applications

An emerging project applying networking and dynamical systems approaches to biological systems, with applications in medicine. Direction is still evolving.
