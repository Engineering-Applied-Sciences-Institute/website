Privacy Policy

How we handle your information on easinstitute.org.

Last updated: March 2026

Scope

This policy applies to the website of Engineering and Applied Sciences Institute (EASI) at easinstitute.org.

What we collect

We collect only what you send via the contact form: name, email address, subject, and message. The form is handled by Formspree; their privacy policy applies to that processing. We do not collect data from mere visits to the site.

How we use it

We use your contact form submission to respond to you. We do not sell, rent, or share your information with third parties.

Retention and your rights

We keep form submissions as long as needed to respond and for reasonable records. You may ask us to access, correct, or delete your data by contacting us via the contact form.

Cookies

This site does not use cookies.

Updates

We may update this page; the “Last updated” date will change when we do.
